template<typename S>
struct momentum::SDFColliderT< S >

SDF collision geometry for character collision detection.

Defined by a transformation, parent joint, and a shared pointer to a SignedDistanceField.

Checks if the current SDF collider is approximately equal to another.

SDF data is compared by pointer identity, not by value: two colliders sharing the same SignedDistanceField instance are considered equal, but two colliders pointing to distinct instances with identical contents are not.
