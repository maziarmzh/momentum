◆ jointParamToFbx()

float momentum::fbx_internal::jointParamToFbx ( float  value,
size_t  curveOffset,
const Joint &  joint 
)
inline

Convert a raw momentum joint parameter to the FBX-native value.

FBX curves use 9 channels per joint: tx,ty,tz (0-2), rx,ry,rz (3-5), sx,sy,sz (6-8). Momentum stores 7 params: translation, rotation (radians), uniform log2-scale.

  • Translation: adds the joint's rest-pose translationOffset.
  • Rotation: converts radians to degrees.
  • Scale: converts log2 to linear (exp2).
Parameters
valueRaw joint parameter value from the motion matrix.
curveOffsetFBX curve channel index (0-8).
jointThe joint providing the translation offset.
